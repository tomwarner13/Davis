Guide to the Davis Papers 

Collection of papers and notes on Lancaster County, 
with many mentions of the Davises.

Jenkin Davies came from County Cardigan, Wales, to Radnor Township, Pennsylvania, around 1719.  After a few years he bought land on Conestoga Creek in Earl Township, and eventually relocated there.  He died in 1747, leaving a large and growing family that spread out over southern Pennsylvania and central Maryland.

 

 

A portrait of Eli Davis, 1809-1887

Eli married Rachel Morsell; together they raised ten children on their farm southeast of Frederick, Maryland. Visit our Genealogy page and read the "Four Generations of Davises" essay below.
